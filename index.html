This tool is my attempt to understand and sort the landscape of people, organizations and businesses who are working to increase bicycle ridership in the US. I hope you will find it useful, and that you might provide feedback and input to make it better.

I've sorted work into high-level categories called "Strategies", followed by more specific categories called "Approaches". When you click on each, you will see a "zoomed in" view of the specific efforts that are being done, and related media coverage. If the way I have organized information doesn't feel quite right, please fill out the feedback form to let me know!
